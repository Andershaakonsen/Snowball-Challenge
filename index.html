Peacekeeping forces

"Peacekeeping has proven to be one of the most effective tools available to the UN to assist host countries navigate the difficult path from conflict to peace." - United Nations Peacekeeping

UN SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOAL 2:

ZERO HUNGER

Our Solution

Our solution to stop world hunger is to prioritize peacekeeping forces. We want globally more contribution to the UN´s peacekeeping forces. We want every country in the world to commit to a more robust and equipped army. The UN´s forces should be able to solve conflicts without worrying about the recruiting and training of these soldiers and military personnel. Their sole focus should be to solve conflicts peacefully and with the consent of all parties involved.

Why it will work

War has often catastrophic consequences. It can destroy the progress of a country in a short period of time. People lose their jobs, their food sources, and it can erase a big portion of the country’s population. Peacekeeping forces has been shown to be a usefull tool to end conflicts around the world for many years now. If we can prevent conflicts from happening, and hopefully stop the current conflicts it would have a great impact on World Hunger.
